Humoroo!

Click here to see the github for this project.

Humoroo! is a webpage that generates a three-panel comic given any two concepts. Through the use of a word to vector model, a third related concept is generated and then used in a humorous punchline ovelaid on top of a series of appropriate images. This project was created on a team as a part of a course taken April - June 2022. Feel free to read through the code.

I'm most proud of the Eureka! moment I had figuring out how to generate the third related word. Inspiration struck while I was playing Semantle!
